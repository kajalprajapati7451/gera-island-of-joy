About The Project

Nestled along the riverbanks & a part of an Integrated Township, Gera's Island of Joy is Pune's most premium ChildCentric® Homes project.

Spread across 12.5 acres of gated development with 33-storey buildings, it features Pune's 1st Go Karting Track & Bowling Alley within Society Premises.

The project boasts a 40,000+ sq. ft. Multi-storey Clubhouse and 4 Acres of Open Spaces with 30+ World-Class Amenities.

Location Highlights:

  • ✓ At East Kharadi, part of an 100+ Acre Integrated Township
  • ✓ 10 mins to Gera Commerzone, WTC & EON IT Park
  • ✓ 10 mins to Oxford World School, Podar International School & Wellington International School
  • ✓ 25 mins to Amanora Mall, Seasons Mall & Phoenix Mall Marketcity
